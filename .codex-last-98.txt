Implemented the newsletter growth widget end to end. The main backend touchpoints are [server.js](/Users/marco/marco_web/server.js), [lib/newsletter.js](/Users/marco/marco_web/lib/newsletter.js), and the schema extension in [db.js](/Users/marco/marco_web/db.js).

On the frontend, I added a reusable EJS partial at [views/partials/newsletter-widget.ejs](/Users/marco/marco_web/views/partials/newsletter-widget.ejs), embedded it on [views/browse.ejs](/Users/marco/marco_web/views/browse.ejs) and [views/agent-detail.ejs](/Users/marco/marco_web/views/agent-detail.ejs), converted the pricing capture on [public/pricing.html](/Users/marco/marco_web/public/pricing.html) to the newsletter flow, and added shared behavior/styles in [public/js/main.js](/Users/marco/marco_web/public/js/main.js) and [public/css/style.css](/Users/marco/marco_web/public/css/style.css). That covers:

- sticky bottom CTA on `/browse` and `/pricing` with “Join 2K+ operators”
