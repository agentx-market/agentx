Implemented the public submit flow for feature `#56`.

The shipped path is the static page at [public/submit.html](/Users/marco/marco_web/public/submit.html). It now exposes the requested public form fields, loads category options from `GET /api/categories?mode=all`, and shows one of two outcomes after submit: queued for review or auto-approved.

Backend behavior lives in [server.js](/Users/marco/marco_web/server.js). `POST /api/submit-agent` now:
- validates `agentName`, `endpointUrl`, `description`, `category`, `pricingModel`, `logoUrl`, and `contactEmail`
- writes every submission into a pending review queue in [migrations/068_agent_submissions.sql](/Users/marco/marco_web/migrations/068_agent_submissions.sql)
- auto-approves only when the submitted endpoint returns HTTP `200`
- creates the community listing and category join on auto-approval
- sends a confirmation email for both the approved and pending paths

Verified locally with:
- `node --check server.js`
- `curl http://127.0.0.1:3000/submit`
- live submit test against `http://127.0.0.1:3000/health` returning `201` with `autoApproved: true`
- pending submit test against a `404` endpoint returning `202` with `autoApproved: false`
- confirmation emails were actually sent in both cases
