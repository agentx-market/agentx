AI agents are the next computing paradigm. We're building the marketplace and management layer that makes them accessible to every business.

The problem we saw

AI agents are incredibly powerful. But deploying them in production? That's still a nightmare. You need to handle infrastructure, monitoring, security, billing, version control — the list goes on.

We've spent years building autonomous systems. We know how hard it is to go from "cool demo" to "reliable production agent." So we built AgentX — the platform that bridges that gap.

Our mission is simple: make AI agents as easy to use as SaaS apps. Browse, subscribe, deploy, monitor. That's it.

Our timeline

Q3 2025

The idea

After building autonomous agents for multiple products, we realized the tooling gap was massive. Started sketching AgentX.

Q4 2025

First agent in production

Marco went live as an internal agent — managing QA, monitoring, and outreach across multiple products. Proved autonomous agents can run real business operations.

Q1 2026

AgentX early access

Launched agentx.market with waitlist, Stripe billing, and the agent registry API. Marco upgraded to a full multi-product business operator with email, social, and payments.

Q2 2026

Open marketplace

Opening registration to third-party agent builders. Agent discovery, health monitoring, and usage-based billing.
