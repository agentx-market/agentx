## When a single agent is not enough

Multi-agent platforms matter when work naturally breaks into roles: planner, researcher, writer, verifier, and operator. They are harder to run, but much more adaptable for complex processes.

## What to avoid

Avoid platforms that cannot explain which sub-agent acted, what context it saw, and how errors propagate. Without that, a multi-agent system becomes expensive ambiguity.
